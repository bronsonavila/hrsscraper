§572B-2 Eligibility to enter into a civil union. A person shall be eligible to enter into a civil union only if the person is:

(1) Not a partner in another civil union or a spouse in a marriage;

(2) At least eighteen years of age; and

(3) Not related to the other proposed partner in the civil union, as provided in section 572B‑3. [L 2011, c 1, pt of §2; am L 2012, c 267, §5]
