§514A-32 Questionnaire and filing fee. The notice of intention shall be accompanied by:

(1) A nonrefundable fee as provided in rules adopted by the director of commerce and consumer affairs pursuant to chapter 91;

(2) A copy of a questionnaire properly filled in; and

(3) Such documents and information concerning the project as may be specified by the commission.

The questionnaire shall be in such form and content as prescribed by the commission. [L 1977, c 98, pt of §2; am L 1988, c 72, §1; am L 1991, c 44, §3; am L 1997, c 40, §23]
