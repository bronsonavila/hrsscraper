§577-24 Escort's responsibility; penalty. Any person who knowingly takes, escorts, or accompanies any unmarried minor to a dance hall which the minor is prohibited from attending by section 577-22, or who invites or encourages the minor to attend such dance hall, shall be fined not more than $100 or imprisoned not more than ninety days. [L 1927, c 224, pt of §1; RL 1935, §4537; RL 1945, §12289; RL 1955, §330-21; HRS §577-24; am L 1975, c 77, §1(8)]
