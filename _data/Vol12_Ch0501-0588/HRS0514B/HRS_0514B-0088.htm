[ยง514B-88] Delivery. In this part, delivery shall be made by:

(1) Personal delivery;

(2) Registered or certified mail with adequate postage, to the recipient's address; provided that delivery shall be considered made three days after deposit in the mail or on any earlier date upon which the return receipt is signed;

(3) Facsimile transmission, if the recipient has provided a fax number to the sender; provided that delivery shall be considered made upon the sender's receipt of automatic confirmation of transmission; or

(4) Any other way prescribed by the commission. [L 2005, c 93, pt of ยง4]
