§505-1 Notices filed where. Notices of liens for internal revenue taxes payable to the United States and certificates of release or of partial discharge of such liens may be recorded in the bureau of conveyances. [L 1931, c 220, §1; RL 1935, §5190; RL 1945, §12790; am L 1949, c 216, §1; RL 1955, §346-1; am L 1966, c 33, §9; HRS §505-1; am L 1973, c 128, §1]

Filing liens, see RLC rule 62.
