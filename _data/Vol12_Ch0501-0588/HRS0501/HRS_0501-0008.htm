§501-8 Registrar may act in any circuit. The registrar may act in any judicial circuit. After lands have been registered under this chapter, the registrar may make all memoranda affecting the title, and enter and issue certificates of title as provided herein. [L 1903, c 56, §9; RL 1925, §3198; RL 1935, §5007; RL 1945, §12607; RL 1955, §342-8; HRS §501-8; gen ch 1985]
