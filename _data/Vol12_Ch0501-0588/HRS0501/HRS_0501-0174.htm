§501-174 Power of attorney; registration necessary. Any person may by attorney procure land to be registered and convey or otherwise deal with registered land, but the letters of attorney shall be acknowledged and filed or recorded with the assistant registrar and registered. Any instrument revoking such letters shall be acknowledged and registered in like manner. [L 1903, c 56, §104; RL 1925, §3294; RL 1935, §5092; RL 1945, §12692; RL 1955, §342-92; HRS §501-174; am L 1986, c 246, §24]
