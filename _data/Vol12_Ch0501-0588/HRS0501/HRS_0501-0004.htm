§501-4 Process. Citations, orders of notice, and all other process issuing from the court shall bear teste of the judge, and be under the seal of the court and signed by the registrar. [L 1903, c 56, §5; am imp L 1913, c 21, §1; RL 1925, §3194; RL 1935, §5003; RL 1945, §12603; RL 1955, §342-4; HRS §501-4]
