§516-124 Acquisition loan programs; fees. The corporation may establish, revise, charge, and collect fees, premiums, and charges as necessary, reasonable, or convenient, for its acquisition loan programs.

The fees, premiums, and charges shall be deposited into the fee title acquisition loan program revenue bond special fund established for the particular acquisition loan program or part thereof from which the fees, premiums, and charges are derived as determined by the corporation. [L 1983, c 270, pt of §1; am L 1987, c 337, §16; am L 1988, c 104, §2; am L 2005, c 196, §26(b); am L 2006, c 180, §16]
