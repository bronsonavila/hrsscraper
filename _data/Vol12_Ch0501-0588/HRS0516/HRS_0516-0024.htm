§516-24 Compensation. The compensation to be paid for the leased fee interest in a residential houselot within a tract shall be the owner's basis as defined in section 516-1. The compensation shall be determined as of the date of the summons of the complaint in eminent domain. [L 1967, c 307, §13; HRS §516-24; am L 1968, c 46, §2e; am L 1975, c 184, §2(8); am L 1983, c 204, §2]
