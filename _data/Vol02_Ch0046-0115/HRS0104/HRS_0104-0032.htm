ยง104-32 Suspension during emergency. During a national emergency declared by the President or the Congress of the United States, or a state of emergency declared by the governor, subject to the provisions of section 127-10 or 128-7, the governor, by executive order in writing, may suspend this chapter; provided that the governor may not suspend this chapter except in the event such an emergency occurs and is so proclaimed.
