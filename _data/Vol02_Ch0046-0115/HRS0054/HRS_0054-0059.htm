§54-59 Audits. The auditor of the county shall make an annual audit of the accounts and financial status of the board of water supply and shall file a copy of the audit report with the council of the county. The board may, whenever necessary in connection with the issuance of any bonds, employ a certified public accountant to make audits. [L 1951, c 171, §1(3); RL 1955, §146-108; HRS §54-59; am L 1998, c 124, §4]
