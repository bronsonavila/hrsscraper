§88-112 Annuity savings fund; annual statement. The annuity savings fund shall be comprised of the members' accumulated contributions, which beginning July 1, 1988, shall include any contributions made by the member to the post retirement fund. A member shall be mailed an annual statement showing the member's accumulated contributions upon request therefor.
