§88-137 Ordinary death benefit. If any service member dies, the service member shall be deemed to be on authorized leave without pay for the purposes of the ordinary death benefit provided in sections 88-84, 88-286(b), and 88-338. [L 1951, c 262, pt of §3; RL 1955, §6-116; HRS §88-127; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985; am L 2004, c 179, §20; am L 2006, c 169, §23]
