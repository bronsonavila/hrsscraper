§88-48 Deduction in class A member's account. An amount equal to the taxes under the Federal Insurance Contributions Act payable by a class A member for the period beginning January1, 1956, and ending on the date class A membership is obtained, shall be deducted from the class A member's account in the system. Any member may elect to contribute to the system an amount equal to the taxes so deducted, which amount shall be credited to the member's account. [L 1957, c 143, §9; Supp, §6-83.01; HRS §88-48; am L 1969, c 110, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]
