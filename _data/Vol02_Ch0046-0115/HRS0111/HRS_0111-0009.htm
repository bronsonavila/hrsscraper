§111-9 Rules and regulations. The Hawaii housing finance and development corporation may adopt and amend rules and regulations to carry out the purposes of this chapter and may adopt all or any part of applicable federal rules and regulations which are necessary or desirable to obtain federal assistance in making payments hereunder. [L 1970, c 166, §9; am L 1987, c 337, §16; am L 1997, c 350, §14; am L 2005, c 196, §26(b); am L 2006, c 180, §16]
