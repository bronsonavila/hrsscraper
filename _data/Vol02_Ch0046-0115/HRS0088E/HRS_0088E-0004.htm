§88E-4 Composition of the board of trustees. The board of trustees shall consist of seven members as follows:

(1) The director of human resources development of the State or a designated representative, ex officio;

(2) The director of finance of the State or a designated representative, ex officio; and

(3) Five other persons, who shall be public employees and represent employee interests. [L Sp 1981 1st, c 7, pt of §1; am L 1988, c 287, §2; am L 1994, c 56, §21; am L 2010, c 65, §1]
