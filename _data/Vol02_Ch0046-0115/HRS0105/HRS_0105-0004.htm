§105-4 Enforcement; duties of sheriffs and police officers. The sheriff and the sheriff's deputies, or any police officer, within their respective jurisdictions, shall investigate any violation, or suspected violation of, and shall enforce section 105-1. [L 1949, c 389, pt of §1(d); RL 1955, §7-13; am L 1963, c 85, §3; HRS §105-4; gen ch 1985]
