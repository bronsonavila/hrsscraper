The symbol of the office of the sergeant-at-arms of each house of the legislature shall be a metal badge in such form as such house shall adopt, bearing the words, among others, "Sergeant-at-Arms of the Senate, State of Hawaii", or "Sergeant-at-Arms of the House of Representatives, State of Hawaii", as the case may be, and shall be displayed by the sergeant-at-arms in enforcing or carrying out the sergeant-at-arms' duties.
