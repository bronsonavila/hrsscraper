[ยง11-421] Hawaii election campaign fund; creation. (a) The Hawaii election campaign fund is created as a trust fund within the state treasury.

(b) The fund shall consist of:

(1) All moneys collected from persons who have designated a portion of their income tax liability to the fund as provided in section 235-102.5(a);

(2) Any general fund appropriations; and

(3) Other moneys collected pursuant to this part.

(c) Moneys in the fund shall be paid to candidates by the comptroller as prescribed in section 11-431 and may be used for the commission's operating expenses, including staff salaries and fringe benefits. [L 2010, c 211, pt of ยง2]
