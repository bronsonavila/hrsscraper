ยง16-43 Ballot handling. In every case where the ballots are handled by election officials or election employees, from the time the ballots are delivered to the several precincts to the time they are returned to the chief election officer or clerk in county elections for disposition upon completion of the tabulation, they shall be handled in the presence of not less than two officials assigned in accordance with sections 11-71 and 11-72 or section 16-45.
