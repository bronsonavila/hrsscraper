DISQUALIFICATION

Section 2. No person who is non compos mentis shall be qualified to vote. No person convicted of a felony shall be qualified to vote except upon the person's final discharge or earlier as provided by law. [Am Const Con 1968 and election Nov5, 1968; am Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]
