ARTICLE IV

REAPPORTIONMENT

Note

This article, entitled "Reapportionment," is new, added by Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978. The former Article IV, entitled "The Executive," now appears as Article V.

REAPPORTIONMENT YEARS

Section 1. The year 1973, the year 1981, and every tenth year thereafter shall be reapportionment years. [Add Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]
