BOARD OF EDUCATION

Section 2. There shall be a board of education. The governor shall nominate and, by and with the advice and consent of the senate, appoint the members of the board of education, as provided by law. [Am HB 4 (1963) and election Nov3, 1964; ren and am Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978; am HB 2688 (1988) and election Nov8, 1988; am HB 2376 (2010) and election Nov 2, 2010]
