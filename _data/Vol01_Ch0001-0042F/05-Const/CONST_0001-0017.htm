RIGHT TO BEAR ARMS

Section 17. A well regulated militia being necessary to the security of a free state, the right of the people to keep and bear arms shall not be infringed. [Ren Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]

Case Notes

Right to bear arms may be regulated by the State in a reasonable manner. 82 H. 143, 920 P.2d 357.
