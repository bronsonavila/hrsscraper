TAX REVIEW COMMISSION

Section 3. There shall be a tax review commission, which shall be appointed as provided by law on or before July1, 1980, and every five years thereafter. The commission shall submit to the legislature an evaluation of the State's tax structure, recommend revenue and tax policy and then dissolve. [Add Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]
