RULES

Section 7. The supreme court shall have power to promulgate rules and regulations in all civil and criminal cases for all courts relating to process, practice, procedure and appeals, which shall have the force and effect of law. [Ren Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]

Attorney General Opinions

This section does not vest exclusive rule making power in the supreme court so as to preclude legislative action on procedural matters. Att. Gen. Op. 67-9.
