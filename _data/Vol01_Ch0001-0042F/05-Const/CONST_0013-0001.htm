ARTICLE XIII

ORGANIZATION; COLLECTIVE BARGAINING

Note

This article was renumbered from Article XII to be Article XIII by Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978. The former Article XIII now appears as Article XV.

PRIVATE EMPLOYEES

Section 1. Persons in private employment shall have the right to organize for the purpose of collective bargaining. [Ren Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]
