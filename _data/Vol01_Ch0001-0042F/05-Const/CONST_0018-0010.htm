DEBTS

Section 10. The debts and liabilities of the Territory shall be assumed and paid by the State, and all debts owed to the Territory shall be collected by the State. [Ren Const Con 1968 and election Nov5, 1968; ren Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]

Case Notes

All territorial general obligation bonds outstanding and unpaid at time of statehood became state obligations. 44 H. 154, 163, 352 P.2d 861.
