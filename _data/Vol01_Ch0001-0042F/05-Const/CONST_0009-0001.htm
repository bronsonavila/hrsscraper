ARTICLE IX

PUBLIC HEALTH AND WELFARE

Note

This article was renumbered from Article VIII to be Article IX by Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978. The former Article IX now appears as Article X.

Law Journals and Reviews

Environmental Protection Based on State Constitutional Law: A Call for Reinterpretation. 12 UH L. Rev. 123.

PUBLIC HEALTH

Section 1. The State shall provide for the protection and promotion of the public health. [Ren Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]

Attorney General Opinions

Enumeration of certain powers by this section was not intended to restrict the legislature. Att. Gen. Op. 68-17.
