EFFECTIVE DATE FOR TERM LIMITATIONS FOR GOVERNOR

AND LIEUTENANT GOVERNOR

Section 4. The amendments to Sections 1 and 2 of Article V shall limit the term of any person elected to the office of governor or lieutenant governor in the 1978 general election to two consecutive full terms commencing from noon on the first Monday in December, 1978. [Add Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]
