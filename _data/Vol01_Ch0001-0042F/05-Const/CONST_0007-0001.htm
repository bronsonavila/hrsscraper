ARTICLE VII

TAXATION AND FINANCE

Note

This article was renumbered from Article VI to be Article VII by Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978. The former Article VII now appears as Article VIII.

TAXING POWER INALIENABLE

Section 1. The power of taxation shall never be surrendered, suspended or contracted away. [Ren Const Con 1978 and election Nov7, 1978]
