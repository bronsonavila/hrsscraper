§209-6 Relation to other agencies. This chapter is not intended, nor shall it be construed in any manner, to conflict with or assume the responsibility of the American National Red Cross, any agency of the federal government, the Salvation Army, or the emergency management activities of the state department of defense. [L 1961, c 189, §7; Supp, §98P-7; HRS §209-6; am L 2014, c 111, §8]
