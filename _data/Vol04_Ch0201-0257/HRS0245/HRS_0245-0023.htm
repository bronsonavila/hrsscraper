§245-23 Department to furnish stamps; designs, specifications, and denominations. The department shall furnish stamps for sale to licensees. Stamps shall be of such designs, specifications, and denominations as may be prescribed by the department. [L 2000, c 249, pt of §1, §20(2); am L 2002, c 94, §3; am L 2004, c 216, §30; am L 2005, c 22, §6]
