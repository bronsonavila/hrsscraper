§201-5 Promotion director and employees. The director of business, economic development, and tourism shall employ a promotion director who shall be experienced in industrial and economic promotional activities, and who shall be exempt from chapter 76. The director of business, economic development, and tourism may employ other persons and the director shall determine their qualifications, duties, and compensations subject to chapter 76. The compensation of the promotion director shall be determined by the director of business, economic development, and tourism.
