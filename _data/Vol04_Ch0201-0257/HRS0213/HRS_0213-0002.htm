§213-2 Personnel. The director of business, economic development, and tourism shall select and designate qualified personnel as the director may deem essential to effectuate the objectives of the research and development industry promotion program. [L 1965, c 210, §3; Supp, §98V-3; HRS §213-2; gen ch 1985; am L 1987, c 336, §7; am L 1990, c 293, §8]
