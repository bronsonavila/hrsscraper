[ยง211F-8] Business and industry evaluation and priorities for job opportunity and economic development. The corporation shall develop procedures to set priorities as to which types of businesses and industries are most likely to provide significant opportunities for economic development and diversification in the State, consistent with the purposes of this chapter. This evaluation shall take into account the guidelines provided by the state plan for economic development.
