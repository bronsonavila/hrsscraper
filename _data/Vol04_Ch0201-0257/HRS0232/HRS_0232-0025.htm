§232-25 Amendment of assessment list to conform to decision. The assessor shall alter or amend the assessment and the assessment list in conformity with the decision or judgment of the last board or court to which an appeal may have been taken. [L 1932 2d, c 40, §56; RL 1935, §1954; am L 1939, c 208, §14; RL 1945, §5220; RL 1955, §116-22; HRS §232-25]
