§486J-2 Distributors to register. Every distributor, and any person before becoming a distributor, shall register as such with the department on forms to be prescribed, prepared, and furnished by the department. [L 1997, c 257, pt of §2; am L 2002, c 77, §4; am L 2006, c 78, §4; am L 2013, c 259, §3]
