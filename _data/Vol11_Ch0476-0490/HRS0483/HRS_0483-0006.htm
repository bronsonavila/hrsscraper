§483-6 Executor or administrator of joint obligor bound. On the death of a joint obligor in contract, the joint obligor's personal representative shall be bound as such jointly with the surviving obligor or obligors. [L 1941, c 60, §6; RL 1945, §8746; RL 1955, §192-6; HRS §483-6; am L 1976, c 200, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]
