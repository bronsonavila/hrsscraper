[ยง489D-6] License qualifications. (a) Each licensee, at all times, shall have a net worth of not less than $1,000, calculated in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles.

(b) Each corporate applicant, at the time of filing an application, and at all times after a license is issued, shall be in good standing in the state of its incorporation.
