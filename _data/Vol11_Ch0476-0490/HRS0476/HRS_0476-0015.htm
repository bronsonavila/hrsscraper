§476-15 Confession of judgment; power of attorney; wage assignment. No provision in a credit sale contract for confession of judgment, power of attorney therefor, or wage assignment shall be enforceable in this State. [L 1961, c 102, pt of §1; Supp, §201A-12; am L 1967, c 285, §5; HRS §476-13; am and ren L 1984, c 86, pt of §1]
