[ยง436H-9] Powers and duties of the director. In addition to any other powers and duties authorized by law, the director shall have the powers and duties to:

(1) Adopt, amend, and repeal rules in accordance with chapter 91 to carry out the purposes of this chapter;

(2) Issue and renew registrations pursuant to this chapter and deny or refuse to renew registrations for failure to comply with this chapter;

(3) Suspend or revoke any registration for any violation of this chapter, chapter 436B, or for any violation of rules adopted by the director pursuant to this chapter;

(4) Establish fees; and

(5) Administer, coordinate, and enforce this chapter. [L 2012, c 198, pt of ยง2]
