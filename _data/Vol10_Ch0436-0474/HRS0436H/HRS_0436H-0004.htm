[ยง436H-4] Registration required. No person shall engage in the practice of athletic training or represent, advertise, or announce oneself, either publicly or privately, as an athletic trainer, nor use in connection with the person's name or place of business the words "registered athletic trainer", "athletic trainer", "ATC", or any other words, letters, abbreviations, or insignia indicating or implying that the person is an athletic trainer unless the person meets the qualifications established by this chapter and has registered with the department.
