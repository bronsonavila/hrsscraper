[ยง461J-10.15] Recordkeeping for continuing competence. (a) Each licensee shall keep and maintain records showing that each course or activity for which credit is claimed has been completed.

(b) These records shall reflect the title of the course or activity, the date taken or completed, and the record of participation.

(c) Each licensee shall retain such documentation for a period of seven years after the course or activity concludes.

(d) Each licensee shall provide copies of such documentation to the board or its designee within fourteen calendar days after being requested to provide such documentation. [L 2013, c 35, pt of ยง1]
