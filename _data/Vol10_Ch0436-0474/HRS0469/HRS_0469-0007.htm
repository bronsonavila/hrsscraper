§469-7 Powers of department to subpoena witnesses, etc. The department of health may subpoena and examine witnesses under oath upon all such charges as may be preferred before it, and the circuit court of the circuit in which the hearing is held shall enforce by proper proceedings the attendance and testimony of witnesses so subpoenaed. [L 1933, c 36, §6; RL 1935, §1455; RL 1945, §3057; RL 1955, §72-7; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §469-7]
