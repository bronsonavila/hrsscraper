§447-6 Prohibited acts; discipline; penalty. (a) In addition to any other actions authorized by law, the board of dental examiners may suspend or revoke any license issued under this chapter or fine a licensee for any cause authorized by law, including but not limited to the following:

(1) Professional misbehavior; or

(2) Any other violation of this chapter or rules adopted pursuant thereto.

(b) Any person who violates any of the provisions of this chapter or any other law, or who fails to comply with any of the requirements or provisions of this chapter or any other law, a penalty for which is not otherwise provided, shall be fined not less than $50, nor more than $250, and each day's violation or failure to comply shall be deemed a separate violation and shall result in a separate fine.
