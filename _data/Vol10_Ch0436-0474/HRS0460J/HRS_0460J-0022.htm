[ยง460J-22] Fumigation job log; preservation and inspection. A licensee shall maintain a log of each fumigation job performed by the licensee in this State. The log shall be in the form required by the regulations of the board and shall be preserved for a period of at least two years, during which time it shall be available at all times during business hours for inspection by the board and its authorized representatives.
