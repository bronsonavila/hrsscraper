§441-32 Delivery of agreement. The cemetery or pre-need funeral authority shall deliver a copy of any contract pertaining to cemetery property, pre-need interment or pre-need funeral services to the person or persons signing it at the time the signature is obtained; provided that only one copy need be delivered to the parties signing as co-tenants. [L 1967, c 199, pt of §1; HRS §441-32; am L 1969, c 242, §13; am L 1985, c 101, §13]
