[ยง441-5.5] Family burial plots. Owners of residential or agricultural property who use or intend to use their property for the interment of family members, shall be exempt from the provisions of this chapter; provided:

(1) Burial plots on the owner's property shall be offered to family members only;

(2) Burial plots shall not be sold or transferred to anyone, except as part of a sale or transfer of all or a substantial portion of the property; and

(3) Any subsequent disinterment of any person interred on the property shall be conducted as though the property were cemetery property under section 6E-41. [L 1996, c 145, ยง2]
