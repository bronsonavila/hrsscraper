§414D-277 Registered agent of foreign corporation. Each foreign corporation authorized to transact business in this State shall continuously maintain in this State a registered agent, who shall have a business address in this State and may be:

(1) An individual who resides in this State;

(2) A domestic entity authorized to transact business in this State; or

(3) A foreign entity authorized to transact business in this State. [L 2001, c 105, pt of §1; am L 2002, c 130, §64; am L 2003, c 124, §37; am L 2009, c 55, §24]
