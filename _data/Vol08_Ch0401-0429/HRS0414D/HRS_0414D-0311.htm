[PART XVI.] SUPERSEDING CHAPTERS

§414D-311 Superseding chapters. [Section effective until December 31, 2018. For section effective January 1, 2019, see below.] In the event of any conflict between the provisions of this chapter and the provisions of chapter 421J, 514A, 514B, or 514E, the provisions of chapter 421J, 514A, 514B, or 514E shall supersede and control the provisions of this chapter. [L 2002, c 130, §3; am L 2004, c 164, §11; am L 2005, c 93, §7; am L 2008, c 28, §8].
