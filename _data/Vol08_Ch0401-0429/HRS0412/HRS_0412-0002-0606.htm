ยง412:2-606 Witness; failure to testify or produce records. Any person who knowingly fails to attend and testify or answer any lawful inquiry or to produce books, papers, accounts, records, contracts, or documents, if in the person's power to do so, in obedience to the lawful subpoena or lawful requirements of the commissioner, or an examiner employed by the commissioner, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor punishable pursuant to sections 706-663 and 706-640.
