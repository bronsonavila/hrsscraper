PART III. LOANS AND EXTENSIONS OF CREDIT

§412:9-300 General requirements for loans and extensions of credit. A financial services loan company shall make loans and extensions of credit that are consistent with prudent lending practices, and in compliance with all applicable federal and state laws. [L 1993, c 350, pt of §1]
