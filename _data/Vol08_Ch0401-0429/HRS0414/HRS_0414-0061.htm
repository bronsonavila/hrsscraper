PART VI. OFFICE AND AGENT

§414-61 Registered agent. Each corporation shall continuously maintain in this State a registered agent, who shall have a business address in this State and may be:

(1) An individual who resides in this State;

(2) A domestic entity authorized to transact business or conduct affairs in this State; or

(3) A foreign entity authorized to transact business or conduct affairs in this State. [L 2000, c 244, pt of §1; am L 2001, c 129, §16; am L 2002, c 130, §11; am L 2009, c 55, §5]
