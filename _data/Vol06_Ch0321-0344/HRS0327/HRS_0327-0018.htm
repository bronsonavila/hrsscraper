[ยง327-18] Immunity. (a) A person that acts in accordance with this part or with the applicable anatomical gift law of another state or attempts in good faith to do so is not liable for the act in a civil action, criminal prosecution, or administrative proceeding.

(b) Neither the person making an anatomical gift nor the donor's estate is liable for any injury or damage that results from the making or use of the gift.

(c) A person who documents the making, amending, or revoking of an anatomical gift under this part may rely upon representations of the individuals listed in section 327-9(a)(2),(3), (4), (5), (6), (7), and (8) relating to their relationship to the donor or prospective donor unless the person knows that the representation is untrue.
