§325-52 Serologic tests; reports. For the purposes of this part a standard serologic test shall be a test for syphilis approved by the department of health and shall be made at a laboratory approved to make such tests by the department. Such laboratory tests as are required by this part shall be made on request without charge at the department laboratories. The department shall issue a "laboratory report form" to be distributed upon application to all laboratories approved to make tests called for in this section. Any laboratory making any such tests shall prepare the report thereof in triplicate. The original of the report shall be transmitted by the laboratory making the test to the certifying physician. The duplicate copy of such report shall be forwarded to the department. The triplicate shall be retained by the laboratory in its files and shall be open at any time for inspection by an authorized representative of the department.
