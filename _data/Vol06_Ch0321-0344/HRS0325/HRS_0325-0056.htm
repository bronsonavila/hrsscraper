§325-56 Penalty. Any physician or other person permitted by law to attend pregnant women, and any other person, who violates this part or any rule of the department of health adopted pursuant to this part, may be fined in an amount not to exceed $1,000 per violation, to be assessed by the director of health. The director of health is authorized to impose the penalty pursuant to this section. [L 1943, c 219, §6; RL 1945, §2315; RL 1955, §49-55; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §325-56; am L 1988, c 158, §3]
