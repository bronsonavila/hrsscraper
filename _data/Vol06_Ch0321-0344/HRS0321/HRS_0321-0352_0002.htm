[ยง321-352.2]Early language services for children who are deaf, hard of hearing, or deaf-blind. Early intervention services for infants and toddlers may include, but not be limited to, service providers and mentors of the deaf, hard of hearing, or deaf-blind who use American Sign Language; service providers and mentors of the deaf who use oral language; American Sign Language teachers for families of young children; family-to-family support; and training of early intervention providers.
