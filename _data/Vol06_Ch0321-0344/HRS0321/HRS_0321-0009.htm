§321-9 Seal, rules and regulations. The department of health may adopt a seal and may adopt such rules and regulations as it may consider expedient for the conduct of its business. The department may amend or revise such rules and regulations from time to time. [RL 1935, pt of §904; am L 1937, c 122, §4; am L 1941, c 18, §1; RL 1945, §2011; RL 1955, §46-11; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §19; HRS §321-9]
