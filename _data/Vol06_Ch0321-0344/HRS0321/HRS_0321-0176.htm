ยง321-176 Biennial review of progress. Every two years, starting January1, 1979, the department of health, on or before January1 of each two-year cycle, shall submit to the legislature and the governor a report setting forth:

(1) A detailed analysis of the progress made toward fulfilling the statewide children's mental health services plan developed under section 321-175; and
