[ยง329D-17] Prohibition of distribution of medical cannabis and medical cannabis products to minors; penalties. (a) A person commits the offense of promoting medical cannabis or medical cannabis products to a minor if the person intentionally or knowingly distributes any amount of cannabis or manufactured cannabis products that came from a dispensary or production center to a minor who is not a registered qualifying patient.

(b) Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of a class B felony. [L 2015, c 241, pt of ยง2; am L 2017, c 170, ยง2]
