§322-35 Amount paid owner. Whenever property is improved under this part and the comptroller sells the property so improved to satisfy the lien established in consequence of the improvement, the owner shall be paid out of the proceeds of the sale an amount not less than the assessed value of the property before its improvement. [L 1911, c 29, §2; RL 1925, §991; RL 1935, §1339; RL 1945, §2730; RL 1955, §47-34; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §12; HRS §322-35]
