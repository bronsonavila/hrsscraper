§653-2 Attachment of salary, etc. The salary, stipend, or wages of the beneficiary may be attached for, and applied in the payment of the beneficiary's debts, in the manner prescribed in this chapter. [L 1890, c 50, §2; am L 1921, c 66, §1; RL 1925, §2847; RL 1935, §4293; RL 1945, §10322; RL 1955, §238-2; HRS §653-2; gen ch 1985]
