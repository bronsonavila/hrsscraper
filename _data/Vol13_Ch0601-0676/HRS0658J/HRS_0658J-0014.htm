[ยง658J-14] Recording of hearing. (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) an arbitration hearing need not be recorded unless:

(1) Otherwise required by law of this State other than this chapter;

(2) Required by the arbitrator;

(3) Provided by the arbitration agreement; or

(4) Requested by a party.
