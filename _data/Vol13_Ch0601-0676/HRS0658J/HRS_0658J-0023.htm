[§658J-23] Enforcement of confirmed award. (a) The court shall enforce an award confirmed under section 658J‑16, including a temporary award, in the manner and to the same extent as any other order or judgment of a court.

(b) The court shall enforce an arbitration award in a family law dispute confirmed by a court in another state in the manner and to the same extent as any other order or judgment from another state. [L 2017, c 113, pt of §1]
