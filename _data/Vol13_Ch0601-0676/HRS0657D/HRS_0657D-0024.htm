[ยง657D-24] Settlement of cases involving stayed proceedings to foreclose mortgage on, resume possession of, or terminate contract for purchase of, personal property. Where a proceeding to foreclose a mortgage upon or to resume possession of personal property, or to rescind or terminate a contract for its purchase, has been stayed as provided in this chapter, the court, unless in its opinion an undue hardship would result to the dependents of the person in the military service, may appoint three disinterested parties to appraise the property and, based upon the report of the appraisers, order that sum, if any, as may be just, paid to the person in military service or the person's dependent, as the case may be, as a condition of foreclosing the mortgage, resuming possession of the property, or rescinding or terminating the contract.
