[ยง667-85] Neutral qualifications; status and liability. [(a)] A neutral shall possess sufficient knowledge in the areas of law, real estate, or finance and shall receive sufficient training to be able to effectuate the purposes of this part.

[(b)] A neutral shall not be liable for any act or omission that occurs in relation to the administration or operation of the mortgage foreclosure dispute resolution program. A neutral shall not be a necessary party to, called as a witness in, or subject to any subpoena duces tecum for the production of documents in any arbitral, judicial, or administrative proceeding that arises from or relates to the mortgage foreclosure dispute resolution program.
