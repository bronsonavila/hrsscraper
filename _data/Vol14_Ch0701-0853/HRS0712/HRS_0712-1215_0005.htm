[ยง712-1215.5] Promoting minor-produced sexual images in the first degree. (1) A person, eighteen years of age or older, commits the offense of promoting minor-produced sexual images in the first degree if the person intentionally or knowingly commands, requests, or encourages a minor to use a computer, cell phone, or any other device capable of electronic data transmission or distribution, to transmit to any person a nude photograph or video of a minor.

(2) For purposes of this section, a "minor" means any person under eighteen years of age.

(3) Promoting minor-produced sexual images in the first degree is a misdemeanor. [L 2012, c 213, pt of ยง1]
