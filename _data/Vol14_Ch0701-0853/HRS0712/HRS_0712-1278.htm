ยง712-1278 Fine, costs, lien on place. Any attorneys' fees, costs, expenses, and fines imposed against any owner of a business, premises or place in any proceedings under this part shall be a lien upon such business, premises, or place, to the extent of the interest of such person therein, enforceable and collectible by execution issued by the order of the court.
