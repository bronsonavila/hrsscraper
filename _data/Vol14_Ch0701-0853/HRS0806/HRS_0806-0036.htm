§806-36 Property owned by corporation. All property, real and personal, whereof any body corporate shall by law have the management, control, or custody, shall, for the purpose of any indictment or proceeding against any other person for any offense committed on or in respect thereof, be deemed to be the property of the body corporate. [L 1876, c 40, §17; RL 1925, §4051; RL 1935, §5510; RL 1945, §10812; RL 1955, §258-22; HRS §711-36; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
