§806-23 Misjoinder. No indictment shall be quashed, set aside, or dismissed, nor shall any demurrer thereto be sustained for misjoinder of parties defendant or for misjoinder of offenses charged; and if either defect exists the court may sever the indictment containing it into separate indictments or into separate counts as shall be proper. [L 1915, c 215, pt of §2; RL 1925, §4047; RL 1935, §5506; RL 1945, §10808; RL 1955, §258-18; HRS §711-23; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
