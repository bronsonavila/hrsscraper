§805-10 Same; summons, service. The summons shall be served by delivering a copy thereof, and showing the original to the president or other head of the corporation, or to the secretary, treasurer, or managing agent thereof. [L 1893-4, c 24, §2; RL 1925, §4014; RL 1935, §5474; RL 1945, §10777; RL 1955, §257-10; HRS §710-10; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1]
