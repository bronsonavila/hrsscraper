[ยง844D-73] No authorization to relieve a person of administrative duty to provide specimens, samples, or print impressions. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a judge is not authorized to relieve a person of the separate administrative duty to provide specimens, samples, or print impressions required by this chapter if a person was found not guilty by reason of insanity or pleads no contest to a qualifying offense as defined in section 844D-31 or is given a deferred acceptance of plea under chapter 853.
