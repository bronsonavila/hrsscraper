COMMENTARY ON ยง704-416.5

Act 69, Session Laws 1983, added this section to this chapter so that a defendant who is acquitted and committed pursuant to this chapter shall be supervised by the adult probation division upon that person's conditional release. The legislature felt that supervision is necessary for the safety and welfare of the individual as well as the community. Senate Standing Committee Report No. 719, House Standing Committee Report No. 506.

Act 230, Session Laws 2006, made technical nonsubstantive amendments to this section.
