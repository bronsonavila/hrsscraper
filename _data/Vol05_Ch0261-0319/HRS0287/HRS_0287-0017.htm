§287-17 Suspension to continue until judgments paid and proof given. The license shall remain so suspended and shall not be renewed, nor shall any such license be thereafter issued in the name of the person, including any such person not previously licensed, unless and until every such judgment is stayed, satisfied in full or to the extent hereinafter provided, and until the person gives proof of financial responsibility subject to the exceptions stated in sections 287-16 and 287-19. [L 1949, c 393, pt of §1; RL 1955, §160-95; HRS §287-17; am L 1982, c 210, §4]
