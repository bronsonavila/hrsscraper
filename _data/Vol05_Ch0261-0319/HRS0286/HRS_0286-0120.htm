ยง286-120 Authority of examiner of drivers to cancel licenses. The examiner of drivers may cancel any driver's license if the examiner determines that the licensee was not entitled to it, failed to give the required or correct information in the licensee's application, or committed fraud in making the licensee's application or in obtaining the license.

Upon cancellation, the licensee shall surrender the licensee's license to the examiner of drivers. [L 1967, c 214, pt of ยง2; HRS ยง286-120; am L 1970, c 164, ยง3; gen ch 1985]
