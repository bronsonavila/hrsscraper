§288-6 Bond prerequisite to license. No license shall be issued in any county under section 445-222 unless and until the applicant therefor deposits with the treasurer or director of finance of the county a policy of insurance or a bond as hereinabove described. [L 1941, c 186, §3; am L 1943, c 171, pt of §1; RL 1945, §7433; RL 1955, §160-145; HRS §288-6]

Note

Section 445-222 referred to in text is repealed.
