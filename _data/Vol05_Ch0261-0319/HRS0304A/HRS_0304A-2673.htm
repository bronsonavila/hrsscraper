[ยง304A-2673] Designation and authorization of university projects, university systems, networks; authorization of revenue bonds. The designation and authorization of construction and maintenance of a university project, university system, or network, and the authorization for issuance of revenue bonds under this subpart shall be by resolution of the board by a majority of all the members of the board then in office. Any resolution may be adopted at the same meeting at which it is introduced and shall take effect immediately upon adoption. [L 2007, c 161, pt of ยง1]
