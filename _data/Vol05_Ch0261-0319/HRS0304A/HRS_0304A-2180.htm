[§304A-2180] Uluulu: The Henry Kuualoha Giugni Moving Image Archive of Hawaii special fund. There is established the Uluulu: The Henry Kuualoha Giugni Moving Image Archive of Hawaii special fund into which shall be deposited any legislative appropriations, federal or private grants, and any other funds collected for the purposes of the Uluulu: The Henry Kuualoha Giugni Moving Image Archive of Hawaii designated under section 304A-1864. The fund shall be administered and expended by the University of Hawaii. Moneys in the fund shall be expended to support the activities of the moving image archive. [L 2012, c 90, §3]
