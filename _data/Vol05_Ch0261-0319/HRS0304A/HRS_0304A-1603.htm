[ยง304A-1603] Labor education advisory council. There is established a labor education advisory council, consisting of not more than fifteen members, broadly representative of the trade union movement in the State, who shall be appointed by the president of the University of Hawaii. The council shall be advisory to the chancellor of the University of Hawaii, West Oahu campus, on all activities and programs of the center for labor education and research and shall assist the chancellor in the assessment and evaluation of program needs for implementation. Members of the council shall designate its chairperson and shall serve without compensation; provided that actual travel and other expenses incurred in the performance of their duties shall be advanced or reimbursed.
