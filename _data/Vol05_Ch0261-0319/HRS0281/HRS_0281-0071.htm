PART V. DUTIES OF AND SUPERVISION OVER LICENSEE

§281-71 Posting of license. Every license issued and in effect under this chapter shall at all times be conspicuously posted and exposed to view, convenient for inspection, on the licensed premises. For failure thereof the license may be suspended or revoked by the liquor commission or liquor control adjudication board. [L Sp 1933, c 40, §37; RL 1935, §2606; RL 1945, §7259; RL 1955, §159-70; HRS §281-71; am L 1998, c 249, §11]
