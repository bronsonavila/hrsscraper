§266-22 Maintenance of fire boat. The cost of operating and maintaining the fire boat shall be borne by the department of transportation. The department shall pay annually from its special fund for the operation and maintenance costs. [L 1951, c 175, §3; RL 1955, §112-22; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §26; am L 1963, c 171, §1; HRS §266-22; am L 2012, c 69, §1]
