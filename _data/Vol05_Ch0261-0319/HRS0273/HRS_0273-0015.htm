§273-15 Bonds. Any corporation which may be organized, and undertake the building of any railroad or railroads, in pursuance of the authority of this chapter, and in accordance with it, may issue bonds to raise money for the construction of such railroad or railroads, in such sums as may be convenient. [L 1878, c 29, §30; RL 1925, §902; RL 1935, §1808; RL 1945, §5066; RL 1955, §106-15; HRS §273-15]
