§261-23 Honolulu International Airport. All that area set aside by executive order No. 1016 for John Rodgers Airport and Keehi Lagoon Seaplane Harbor to be under the control and management of the superintendent of public works under date of April12, 1943, is designated as "Honolulu International Airport". [L 1947, c 31, §1; am L 1951, c 3, §1; RL 1955, §15-27; HRS §261-23]
