§372-7 Apprenticeship committee. Subject to the review of the director and in accordance with the standards established by this chapter and rules adopted by the director, apprenticeship committees shall give assistance to the operation and further development of apprenticeship in their respective occupation and localities. [L Sp 1941, c 23, §3; RL 1945, §4144; RL 1955, §89-7; am L 1967, c 20, §1(f); HRS §372-7; am L 2012, c 14, §8]
