ยง386-209 Monetary penalties. After notice and opportunity for a hearing, the insurance commissioner may impose a monetary penalty on any person or group found to be in violation of any provision of this chapter or any rule adopted thereunder. The monetary penalty shall not exceed $1,000 for each act or violation and shall not exceed $10,000 in the aggregate. The amount of any monetary penalty shall be paid to the insurance commissioner and transmitted to the director of finance for deposit into the general fund. [L 1986, c 304, pt of ยง1]
