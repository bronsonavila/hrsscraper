[§346G-11] Special designation of hospital sustainability program special fund. [See Note at chapter heading.] Notwithstanding section 37-53, and any law or any administrative rule to the contrary, the specific purposes set out in section 346G-4(c) are established as being exclusive uses of the hospital sustainability program special fund. The hospital sustainability program special fund shall not and may not be used for any other purposes, notwithstanding any authority granted to the governor or any other state official by any other statutory provisions relating to the allocation or reallocation of funds.
