PART II. BENEFITS

§383-21 Payment of benefits. All benefits provided herein shall be payable from the fund. All benefits shall be paid through employment offices, in accordance with such regulations as the department of labor and industrial relations may prescribe. [L 1939, c 219, §3(b); am L 1941, c 304, §1, pt of subs 15; RL 1945, §4214; RL 1955, §93-20; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §27; HRS §383-21]
