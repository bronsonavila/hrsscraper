§350-5 Admissibility of evidence. The physician-patient privilege, the psychologist-client privilege, the spousal privilege, and the victim-counselor privilege shall not be grounds for excluding evidence in any judicial proceeding resulting from a report of child abuse or neglect pursuant to this chapter. [L 1967, c 261, §6; HRS §350-5; am L 1987, c 204, §8; am L 1992, c 217, §3]
