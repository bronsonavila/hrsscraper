[ยง431P-6] Advisory committee. To assist it in implementing this chapter the fund may appoint an advisory committee consisting of:

(1) Not less than one individual who is employed or trained as a meteorologist and possesses knowledge of the history, trends, and nature of windstorms in the Pacific Ocean;

(2) Not less than one individual who is a member of the American Academy of Actuaries; and

(3) Not less than one individual who is a structural engineer licensed to practice in the State and is knowledgeable about local community building codes.
