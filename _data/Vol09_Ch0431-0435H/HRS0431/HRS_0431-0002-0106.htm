The commissioner, deputies and employees of the insurance division shall not represent, be employed by, own any securities of, be a creditor of, or be financially interested in any other manner in, any insurer authorized to do business in this State, or in any insurance agency in this State, except that the commissioner, or any deputy or employee, may be a policyholder or obligee of any such insurer.
