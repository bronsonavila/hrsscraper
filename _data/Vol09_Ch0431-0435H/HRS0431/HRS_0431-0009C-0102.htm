§431:9C-102 Licensure. (a) No person, firm, association, or corporation shall act as a managing general agent, with respect to risks located in this State for an insurer licensed in this State, unless licensed as a producer in this State.

(b) No person, firm, association, or corporation shall act as a managing general agent representing an insurer domiciled in this State with respect to risks located outside this State unless licensed as a producer in this State. [L 2002, c 155, pt of §2; am L 2003, c 212, §70; am L 2006, c 189, §7; am L 2010, c 116, §1(10)]
