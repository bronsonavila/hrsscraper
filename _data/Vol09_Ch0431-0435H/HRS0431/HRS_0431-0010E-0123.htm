[§431:10E-123] Definitions. As used in this part, "inquiry" means a telephone call or other communication made to an insurer regarding the terms, conditions, or coverage afforded under an insurance policy that does not result in a claim, including questions concerning whether a policy will cover a loss or the process for filing a claim. The term shall not constitute a claim for purposes of section 431:13‑103(a)(11). [L 2012, c 80, pt of §1]
