ยง431:6-404 Authorization of investments. No investment, loan, sale, or exchange, except a loan upon a life insurance policy, shall be made by any domestic insurer unless authorized or approved by its board of directors or by a committee charged by the board of directors, or the bylaws with the duty of making such investment, loan, sale, or exchange. The minutes of any such committee shall be recorded and reports shall be submitted to the board of directors for approval or disapproval. [L 1987, c 347, pt of ยง2]
