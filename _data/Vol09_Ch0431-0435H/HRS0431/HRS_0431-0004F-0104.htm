[ยง431:4F-104] Maintenance of trust account. The trusteed assets, or the assets of the trust account of an alien insurer, as required by section 431:4F-103, shall at all times be in an amount equal to the United States branch's reserves and other liabilities plus the minimum capital and surplus or authorized control level risk-based capital, whichever is greater, required to be maintained by a domestic insurer licensed to do the same kind of insurance.
