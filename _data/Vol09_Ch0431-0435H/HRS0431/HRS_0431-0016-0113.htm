ยง431:16-113 Prevention of insolvencies. To aid in the detection and prevention of insurer insolvencies:

(1) The board of directors, upon majority vote, may make recommendations to the commissioner on matters generally related to improving or enhancing regulation for solvency; and

(2) At the conclusion of any domestic insurer insolvency in which the association was obligated to pay covered claims, the board of directors may prepare a report on the history and causes of the insolvency, based on the information available to the association, and submit the report to the commissioner.
