[ยง431S-4] Annual renewal requirement. (a) Each pharmacy benefit manager shall renew its registration by March 31 each year.

(b) When renewing its registration, a pharmacy benefit manager shall submit to the commissioner the following:

(1) An application for renewal on a form prescribed by the commissioner; and

(2) A renewal fee of $140.

(c) Failure on the part of a pharmacy benefit manager to renew its registration as provided in this section shall result in a penalty of $140 and may cause the registration to be revoked or suspended by the commissioner until the requirements for renewal have been met. [L 2017, c 44, pt of ยง2]
