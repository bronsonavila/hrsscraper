[ยง432G-19] Coordination of benefits. (a) Dental insurers are required to adopt provisions for coordination of benefits to avoid overinsurance and to provide for the orderly payment of claims when a person is covered by two or more group health insurance or health care plans.

(b) Provisions adopted pursuant to subsection (a) for the coordination of benefits shall be consistent with the coordination of benefits provisions that are in general use in the State for coordinating coverage between two or more group health insurance or health care plans.
