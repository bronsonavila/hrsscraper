§123-7 Battalions and divisions. A battalion of naval militia shall be considered the equivalent of a battalion of land militia, and a division of naval militia shall be considered the equivalent of a company of land militia and equal in the state service, and they shall be entitled to equal rights, privileges, and allowances when not inconsistent with law. [L 1915, c 151, pt of §7; RL 1925, §278; RL 1935, §7866; RL 1945, §13086; RL 1955, §354-7; HRS §123-7]
