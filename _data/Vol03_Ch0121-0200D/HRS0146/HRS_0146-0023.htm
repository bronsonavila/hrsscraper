§146-23 Duty of vendor of butchered beef to disclose identity of person from whom obtained. It shall be the duty of every vendor of butchered beef to know and truthfully state to any inquirer the name and residence of the person from whom the vendor obtained the beef which the vendor has for sale. [L 1923, c 7, §2; RL 1925, §4189; RL 1935, §5981; RL 1945, §11381; RL 1955, §290-3; HRS §146-23; gen ch 1985]
