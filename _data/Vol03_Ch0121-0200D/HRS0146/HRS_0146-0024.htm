§146-24 Violations; penalty. A violation of section 146-21, 146-22, or 146-23 shall be punishable by a fine of not more than $500 or imprisonment of not more than one year, or both. [L 1923, c 7, §3; RL 1925, §4190; RL 1935, §5982; RL 1945, §11382; RL 1955, §290-4; HRS §146-24; am L 1986, c 163, §3]
