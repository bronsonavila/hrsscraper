§182-14 Rules. Subject to chapter 91, the board may adopt and amend rules as it deems necessary to carry out this chapter and to perform its duties thereunder, all commensurate with and for the purpose of protecting the public interest. All rules shall have the force and effect of law. [L 1963, c 11, pt of §1; Supp, §99A-14; HRS §182-14; am L 2016, c 220, §9]
