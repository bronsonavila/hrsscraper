§142-64 On unfenced cultivated land. If any of the animals mentioned in section 142-63 trespasses on any unfenced cultivated ground, the owner thereof shall pay upon proof, the full amount of the damage or loss to the landowner or to any person in possession of the land, whoever suffers the damage or loss. [L 1907, c 125, §13; RL 1925, §701; RL 1935, §279; RL 1945, §1085; RL 1955, §20-63; HRS §142-64; am L 1975, c 40, pt of §1]
