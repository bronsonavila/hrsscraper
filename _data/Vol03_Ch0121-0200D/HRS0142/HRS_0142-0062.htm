§142-62 Breaking, etc., of fence; penalty. If any person maliciously and designedly opens a gateway, or breaks a fence, so as to allow any animal ingress or egress, so that it may commit a trespass, or maliciously and designedly drives or leads any animal into another locality where it may commit a trespass, the person shall, for every offense, be fined not more than $100, or imprisoned not more than one year. [L 1907, c 125, §11; RL 1925, §699; RL 1935, §277; RL 1945, §1083; RL 1955, §20-61; HRS §142-62; gen ch 1985]
