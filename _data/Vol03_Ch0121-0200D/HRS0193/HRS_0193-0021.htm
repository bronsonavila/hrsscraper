PART III. YOUTH EMPLOYMENT PROGRAM

§193-21 Authorization. The governor is authorized to avail the State of the benefits of any law or laws of the United States, now existing or to be enacted, which will enable young persons to work for state, local, and private nonprofit agencies in programs related to the public interest such as hospital, education, or welfare activities as well as state conservation projects. [L 1963, c 74, §9; Supp, §98N-22; HRS §193-21; am L 1980, c 97, §1]
