[ยง128D-9] Injunctive relief. The director may institute a civil action in any environmental court of competent jurisdiction for injunctive relief to prevent any violation of this chapter, of any rule adopted pursuant to this chapter, or of any order issued pursuant to this chapter. The environmental court shall grant relief in accordance with the Hawaii rules of civil procedure. [L 1990, c 298, pt of ยง17; am L 2014, c 218, ยง8]
