§147-96 Public hearings. Before adopting any rules or regulations authorized by this part, the department of agriculture shall first hold a public hearing upon the proposed rules or regulations on each island. [L 1955, c 272, §5; RL 1955, §22-94; ren L 1957, c 172, §1(d); am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §22; am L 1961, c 132, §2; HRS §147-96]
