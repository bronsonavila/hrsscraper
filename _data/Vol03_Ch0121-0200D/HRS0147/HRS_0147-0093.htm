§147-93 Cooperating with federal authority. The department of agriculture may enter into cooperative agreements with the United States Department of Agriculture for the purpose of grading beef, pork, mutton, and lamb carcasses. [L 1957, c 172, §1(c); am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §22; am L 1961, c 132, §2; Supp, §22-92; HRS §147-93; am L 2003, c 49, §6]
