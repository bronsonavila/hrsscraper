§180-14 State agencies to cooperate. Agencies of the state government, or other governmental subdivisions of the State, which have jurisdiction over publicly owned lands lying within any soil and water conservation district, may cooperate with the district in the effectuation of programs and operations of the district. District directors shall be given access to enter and perform work upon the lands. [L 1947, c 191, §8; RL 1955, §28-17; am L 1961, c 53, §1; HRS §180-14]
