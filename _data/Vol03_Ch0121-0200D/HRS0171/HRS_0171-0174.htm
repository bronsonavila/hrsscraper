[ยง171-174] Lease of Turtle Bay lands. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, the board of land and natural resources may, without public auction, lease lands purchased in fee simple pursuant to section 171-171, to the grantor of the conservation easement or its successor in interest. The purpose of the lease shall be for the protection, preservation, and enhancement of natural resources, while maintaining public access. The lease rental shall be on a nominal basis, shall not exceed a term of sixty-five years, and be upon such other terms and conditions as the board may determine. [L 2015, c 121, pt of ยง2]
